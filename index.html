Our Story

Started in one kitchen. Cooked for every table since.

Ummi's Table began the way most good food does — with one woman, one stove, and a habit of cooking enough for twenty even when only five were coming. Neighbors started asking for trays. Then it was weddings. Then offices. Then birthdays.

We still cook the way she taught us: whole ingredients, real spices toasted by hand, rice that rests before it's served, and nothing rushed or processed. Every order is prepared fresh for your date — halal, but built to be enjoyed by any guest at any table.

Made with love. Seasoned with dua.
